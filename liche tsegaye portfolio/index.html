dice game

This is a two-player dice game where each player takes turns rolling a die.If a player rolls a 1, they lose their turn and any points from that round aren't added to their total score. Any roll other than 1 will be added to that player’s score for the round. Players can decide to "hold" and keep their points for that round, or risk rolling again. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
